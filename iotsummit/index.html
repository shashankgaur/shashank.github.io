About

The 2016 IEEE Tech Industry Summit will be a unique event to bring together leading industries and academic researchers and lead a discussion on various issues for future of Internet of Things (IoT). IEEE Communication Society is a partner of this summit and will organize a workshop session at this summit. This Workshop session will mainly focus on how different types of business are currently engaged into challenges related to IoT. The aim of the workshop will have focus on End-users of Industry. It will highlight what are new products or services currently being used by end users in different industries and what is current status of the IoT setup in end-user ecosystem. The target audience for this event is end-users who are planning or already using IoT technologies for their applications such as, Developers, Start-ups, or public/private entities such as banks, hospitals, etc.
